What is Signal-to-Noise Ratio (SNR)?

Current Technology Landscape

Positioning this system against established optical storage testing practices and emerging AI-driven methodologies.

Traditional SNR Measurement: Optical storage quality is commonly validated through direct signal acquisition and post-process SNR computation under controlled lab conditions.
Hardware Signal Testing & Calibration: Conventional workflows depend on oscilloscopes, photodetector chains, calibrated laser modules, and repeated bench-level calibration procedures.
Limitations of Conventional Testing: Physical setups are cost-intensive, time-consuming, and constrained in exploring broad multidimensional parameter spaces.
Role of DSP in Optical Systems: Digital Signal Processing remains central for filtering, equalization, denoising, and symbol-level interpretation in optical channels.
Emerging AI/ML Adoption: Optical engineering increasingly incorporates machine learning for pattern discovery, inverse modeling, and predictive error minimization.
